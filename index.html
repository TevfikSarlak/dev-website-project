Description: Fridgezy is an innovative web application designed to simplify the cooking experience ... for users by utilizing the ingredients available in their refrigerators. With Fridgezy, users can easily input the items they have in their fridge, and the app provides personalized recipe suggestions based on those ingredients. It's a handy tool that helps users make the most out of what they have, reducing food waste and inspiring creativity in the kitchen. Fridgezy goes beyond just recipe recommendations. It also keeps track of unused ingredients, making it easy for users to identify items that need to be used before they expire. In addition to its core functionality, Fridgezy has exciting upcoming features planned, including the ability to upload custom recipes and the option to add fridge items by simply taking a snapshot with your mobile device.
